About

Hello! My name is David Robideau. I'm a senior in computer engineering (with a focus on software systems) at Iowa State University. I live in Ames, Iowa, where I'm a software engineering intern at Parametric Studio, Inc. I'm a virtual reality enthusiast, avid music fan, and all-around nerd. If you'd like to get in touch with me or get more info about my credentials, click here.

Most of my professional work has been with the Unity 3D engine. I have a deep passion for virtual reality technology, and I taught myself how to use the engine along with C# as a means to develop my own VR applications. In the future, I'd like to secure a position that enables me to use both my low-level computing knowledge and my in-engine skills to work on consumer VR applications.
