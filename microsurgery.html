VR Microsurgery Training

Project Plan - .doc

---

VR Microsurgery Training (name pending) is my capstone design project. Our team's goal is to provide an intuitive platform for training, practice, and performance review of common microsurgery operations in virtual reality. Development of the system began in September 2016 and will conclude in April 2017. The platform will make use of the HTC Vive headset's unique room-tracking abilities and haptic feedback motors to create an accurate full-scale representation of an operating room, a patient, and any necessary tools.

---

I'm serving as one of two key concept holders on this project. My job is to communicate technical expectations for the project and ensure that our development cycle leads to a product resembling our initial vision. More information about our team's goals and progress can be found in the project plan.
