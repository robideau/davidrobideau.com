Oil Change

Demo video - YouTube

---

Oil Change is a semi-synchronous multiplayer game built in the Unity 3D engine. Two players compete to build increasingly complex race tracks and beat their opponent's completion times. Oil Change features a robust multiplayer suite including an in-game chat system, leaderboards, and matchmaking.

---

Oil Change was developed as a semester-long project in my ComSci 309 (software development practices) class. I acted as the lead in-engine developer on this project, while my teammates (Nick Bramanti, Ryan Young, and John Kirchner) handled networking functionality and asset generation. A finished build of the game is included in the repository - if you're interested in giving it a shot, send me an email and I'll start up a server!
